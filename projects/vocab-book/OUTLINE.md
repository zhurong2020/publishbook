## 章节内容规范

每个词汇单元应包含：

1. **单词** (Word)
2. **音标** (Phonetic)
3. **词性** (Part of Speech)
4. **中文释义** (Chinese Definition)
5. **英文释义** (English Definition)
6. **例句** (Example Sentences) - 至少2个
7. **搭配** (Collocations)
8. **派生词** (Derivatives)
9. **记忆技巧** (Memory Tips) - 可选
10. **易混词** (Easily Confused Words) - 可选
